## 💡 How to Use Each Feature

### 1️⃣ Resume Optimizer
- Upload job description + your resume
- AI analyzes requirements
- Generates ATS-optimized resume
- Download in TXT or DOCX format

### 2️⃣ Cover Letter Generator
- Upload job description (optional: resume)
- Fill in your details (name, company, etc.)
- AI creates personalized letter
- Download and customize

### 3️⃣ Interview Prep
- Upload job description
- Select your level (entry/mid/senior)
- Get 15+ role-specific questions
- Includes STAR method guidance

### 4️⃣ Portfolio Generator
- Add your projects and details
- AI creates professional descriptions
- Get Markdown file
- Use on GitHub, LinkedIn, website
