## 📝 How to Use

### 1. Optimize Your Resume
1. Click "📄 Optimize Resume" in the sidebar
2. Upload your job description file
3. Upload your current resume
4. (Optional) Add GPA and coursework
5. Click "🚀 Optimize Resume"
6. Download your tailored, ATS-optimized resume!

### 2. Generate Cover Letter
1. Click "✉️ Cover Letter" in the sidebar
2. Upload job description
3. Fill in your details (name, company, position, email)
4. Click "✍️ Generate Cover Letter"
5. Download your personalized cover letter!

### 3. Prepare for Interviews
1. Click "❓ Interview Prep" in the sidebar
2. Upload job description
3. Select job level (entry/mid/senior)
4. Click "📝 Generate Interview Questions"
5. Download comprehensive prep guide!

### 4. Build Your Portfolio
1. Click "🎨 Portfolio Generator" in the sidebar
2. Fill in your information and projects
3. Click "🎨 Generate Portfolio"
4. Download Markdown file for GitHub/website!

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### For Resume Optimization
- ✅ Use clear, well-formatted input resume
- ✅ Provide complete job description
- ✅ Add GPA if >= 3.0
- ✅ List relevant coursework

### For Cover Letters
- ✅ Research the company beforehand
- ✅ Be specific about why you're interested
- ✅ Mention relevant projects
- ✅ Show enthusiasm

### For Interview Prep
- ✅ Practice answers using STAR method
- ✅ Prepare 3-5 strong examples
- ✅ Research the company thoroughly
- ✅ Have questions ready to ask
